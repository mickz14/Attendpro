Our system offers tailored features for different roles to ensure efficient management. Teachers can mark attendance and view student data, while students can track their attendance records. Head Of Department have access to reports. This role-based approach ensures that each user sees only what's relevant to their responsibilities, enhancing security and usability.

Our system provides real-time access to attendance reports, allowing users to monitor trends and identify issues as they arise.This feature enhances decision-making and helps maintain optimal attendance levels.

Generate Reports
